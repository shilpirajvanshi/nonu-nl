Baby Safety Tips

🌡️

Check temperatures

Always test bath water and baby food temperature before use. Aim for 37°C for baths.

😴

Safe sleep

Always place your baby on their back to sleep, on a firm mattress, in their own space.

🧴

Gentle products

Choose fragrance-free, hypoallergenic products for newborn skin. Patch test new products first.
